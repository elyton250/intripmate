How It Works

Three steps to a connected transport ecosystem

From first booking to final invoice — every touchpoint is seamless, tracked, and compliant.

01

Onboard & Connect

Passengers, drivers, and fleet owners register in minutes. Documents are verified, vehicles assigned, and fuel cards issued — all in one dashboard.

02

Ride & Refuel

Passengers book rides with fare transparency. Drivers navigate with GPS, accept trips, and refuel using controlled fuel cards with real-time balance tracking.

03

Pay & Report

Payments process automatically. VAT-compliant invoices generate on the fly. Fleet owners get consolidated reports; admins get full platform visibility.
